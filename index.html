Introduction

Meri Parampara ("we," "our," or "the app") is a cultural and devotional Android application that provides Hindu calendar (Panchang), festival information, vrat vidhi (fasting rituals), and spiritual content. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • User Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials (stored securely)
  • Personal Notes: Notes and reminders you create within the app are stored locally on your device
  • Custom Alerts: Festival reminders and custom notifications you configure

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting
  • Location Data: Your manually selected location (city/region) for accurate Panchang calculations - this is NOT automatically tracked
  • Usage Data: App features you use and interaction patterns to improve the user experience

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide accurate Panchang (Hindu calendar) calculations based on your location
  • Send you festival reminders and vrat notifications you've configured
  • Save your personal notes and preferences on your device
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All personal data (notes, preferences, reminders) is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Currently, we do not sync your data to any cloud servers
  • Secure Authentication: If account features are enabled, passwords are encrypted using industry-standard security practices
  • No Selling of Data: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

Permissions We Request

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you festival reminders and vrat alerts you've configured
  • Storage: To save your notes, preferences, and downloaded content locally
  • Internet: To display deity images and festival information (if applicable)
  • Alarm/Schedule Exact Alarms: To trigger reminders at precise times for vratas and festivals

Note: All permissions are optional and requested only when needed. You can deny permissions and still use core app features.

Third-Party Services

Currently, the app operates independently without active third-party integrations. If we integrate services in the future (such as analytics or crash reporting), we will update this policy and notify users.

Children's Privacy

Meri Parampara is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all data stored within the app
  • Delete Your Data: Clear all local data through the app settings or by uninstalling the app
  • Control Notifications: Disable or customize reminders at any time
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the app at any time by uninstalling it

Data Retention

  • Personal data is retained until you delete it from the app or uninstall the application
  • Device logs are retained temporarily for troubleshooting and are automatically deleted
  • No data is retained on external servers (as we don't currently use cloud storage)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying an in-app notification (for major changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
